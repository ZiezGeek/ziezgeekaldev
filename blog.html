Phone repair guides and tech help

Why Your Phone Is Not Charging

If your phone is not charging, the problem is usually a damaged charging cable, dust inside the charging port, or a faulty battery. Try cleaning the charging port gently and testing a different charger. If the problem continues, a technician may need to repair the charging port.

How to Fix Android Stuck on Boot Logo

When an Android phone is stuck on the boot logo, it may be caused by software corruption or a failed update. You can try restarting the device, booting into recovery mode, or reinstalling firmware.

When Should You Replace Your Phone Battery?

If your battery drains quickly, overheats, or the phone shuts down randomly, it may be time for a battery replacement. Most phone batteries last 2–3 years depending on usage.
