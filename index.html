About the Founder

Nkosinathi Nhlanhla Sishuba Dlamini – Founder of ZiezGeek

Born on 7 July 2002 in Vosloorus, Gauteng South Africa.

He attended Zonkizizwe Primary School and later Rebontsheng Primary School before completing matric at Erasmus Monareng Secondary School.

His passion for technology started in Grade 9 where he began fixing and unlocking devices. Today he runs ZiezGeek Aldevinc providing repairs, accessories and tech services.
